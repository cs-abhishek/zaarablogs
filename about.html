My Values

My Values

Authenticity

Your business should look and feel like you. I believe in building brands and offers that reflect your personality, style, and values — not what the latest “guru” says you should do.

Impact

It’s not just about making money (though I love helping you do that too). It’s about creating real, tangible results for your clients and yourself. I believe your work can change lives — and I’ll help you make sure it does.

Freedom

I’m here to help you design a business that gives you more life, not less. That means creating systems, boundaries, and offers that support your schedule, your energy, and your priorities — so you can take Fridays off, go for long lunches, or book that trip without stressing.

Growth Mindset

Success isn’t about getting everything perfect the first time — it’s about learning, adapting, and growing through every stage of your business. I’ll always encourage my clients to try, test, and evolve.

My journey

2011

The Creative Spark

I started my career as a photographer, fueled by passion and excitement for creating beautiful work. I loved the craft, but I had no idea how to run the business side of things.

2019

The Breakthrough

I put my new strategies into action. I raised my rates, started saying “no” to the wrong clients, and redesigned my client experience. Within months, I was booking my dream clients — at prices that allowed me to work less and earn more.

2025

Zaara, The Business Coach

I launched my coaching business, combining mindset work, marketing strategy, and a whole lot of encouragement. I also started The Creative Business Podcast to share these lessons with a bigger audience.

2035

Today — Your Turn

Now, I coach service-based creatives all over the world, helping them attract dream clients, earn more, and actually enjoy the business they’ve built. And I’d love to help you do the same.
